Ready to set up AI for your business but not sure where to start?

This guide walks you through the entire process, from choosing a platform to your first automated conversation. Most businesses can be up and running in an afternoon.

## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

1. **30-60 minutes** of focused time
2. **Access to your calendar** (Google, Outlook, or other)
3. **Your FAQ list** (common customer questions)
4. **Your service/pricing info** (for AI to reference)
5. **Your preferred response style** (formal, casual, friendly)

By the end of this guide, you'll have:
- AI responding to inquiries instantly
- Automatic appointment scheduling
- FAQ handling around the clock
- Follow-up sequences running

Let's do this.

## Step 2: Basic Configuration

### Create Your Account

1. Sign up with your business email
2. Verify your email address
3. Complete basic profile (business name, industry)

### Connect Communication Channels

**Website chat:**
- Copy the widget code
- Paste into your website header
- Or use plugin for WordPress/Shopify

**SMS/Text:**
- Verify your business phone number
- Or get a dedicated number from the platform

**Social media:**
- Connect Facebook page
- Connect Instagram business account
- Authorize messaging permissions

**Tip:** Start with one channel. Add more once you're comfortable.

## Step 3: Train Your AI

This is the most important step. Good training = good results.

### Business Information

Enter your details:

```
Business name: [Your Business Name]
Industry: [Your Industry]
Location: [City, State]
Service area: [Areas you serve]
Hours: [Your operating hours]
Contact: [Phone, email]
```

### Services and Pricing

List what you offer:

```
Service: Residential Cleaning
Description: Full house cleaning including all rooms, bathrooms, kitchen
Price: $150-300 depending on size
Duration: 2-4 hours
```

Repeat for each service.

### Common Questions (FAQ)

List your top 20 most-asked questions with answers:

```
Q: What areas do you service?
A: We serve [Area 1], [Area 2], and [Area 3]. If you're unsure, just give me your zip code and I'll confirm.

Q: How much does [service] cost?
A: [Service] typically ranges from $X to $Y depending on [factors]. For an exact quote, I'd need to know [qualifying info].

Q: Do you offer free estimates?
A: Yes! We offer free on-site estimates. I can schedule one at your convenience.
```

### Your Voice and Tone

Tell the AI how to communicate:

```
Tone: Friendly and professional
Use: Conversational language
Avoid: Overly formal or stiff
Examples:
- Good: "I'd be happy to help with that!"
- Avoid: "Your inquiry has been received and will be processed."
```

## Step 4: Set Up Scheduling

### Connect Your Calendar

1. Go to integrations/settings
2. Select your calendar type
3. Authorize access
4. Confirm connection

### Configure Availability

**Days and times:**
- Monday-Friday: 9 AM - 5 PM
- Saturday: 10 AM - 2 PM
- Sunday: Closed

**Buffer time:**
- 15 minutes between appointments
- No same-day booking (if needed)
- Maximum 30 days out

**Appointment types:**
```
Type: Free Consultation
Duration: 30 minutes
Buffer: 15 min before, 15 min after
Available: Mon-Fri, 10 AM - 4 PM
```

### Booking Confirmations

Create confirmation message:

```
You're confirmed for [Appointment Type] on [Date] at [Time].

Details:
- Location: [Address or "Virtual - link to follow"]
- Duration: [Duration]
- What to bring: [If applicable]

I'll send you a reminder 24 hours before. If you need to reschedule, just let me know!
```

## Step 5: Create Response Flows

### Greeting Message

When someone starts a conversation:

```
Hi there! 👋 Thanks for reaching out to [Business Name].

I'm here to help with questions, scheduling, or anything else you need.

What can I help you with today?
```

### Lead Capture Flow

When someone shows interest:

```
AI: Great, I'd love to tell you more about [service]! 

To give you the most relevant information, what's your name?

[Customer answers]

AI: Nice to meet you, [Name]! And what's the best email to send information to?

[Customer answers]

AI: Perfect. And just to make sure we can help, what's your zip code?

[Customer answers]

AI: Great news, [Name]! We serve your area. Here's what you should know about [service]:
[Key info]

Would you like to schedule a free consultation to discuss your specific needs?
```

### Objection Handling

```
[If customer says "too expensive"]
AI: I completely understand budget is important. Our pricing includes [value points]. 

We also offer [payment plans/alternatives]. Would it help to discuss options that might work better for your budget?

[If customer says "just looking"]
AI: No problem at all! Take your time. 

Would you like me to send you some information you can review when you're ready? I can also check back in a few weeks if that's helpful.

[If customer says "working with someone else"]
AI: Totally understand! If things change or you'd like a second opinion, I'm here. 

Is there anything else I can help you with today?
```

## Step 6: Test Everything

### Test Scenarios

Before going live, test:

1. **Basic greeting**: Start a conversation
2. **FAQ response**: Ask a common question
3. **Scheduling**: Book an appointment
4. **Rescheduling**: Change an appointment
5. **Lead capture**: Go through qualification
6. **Edge cases**: Ask something unusual
7. **Handoff**: Request to speak to human

### What to Check

- Are responses accurate?
- Does scheduling work correctly?
- Is the tone right for your brand?
- Do calendar bookings appear?
- Are confirmations sent?

### Adjustments

Based on testing, adjust:
- Response wording
- Timing of messages
- Information completeness
- Escalation triggers

## Step 7: Go Live

### Soft Launch

Start with low-risk channels:
1. Add to website (visitors are already interested)
2. Monitor conversations closely
3. Jump in if AI struggles
4. Note improvements needed

### Full Launch

Once confident:
1. Connect all channels
2. Enable SMS/social
3. Add to email signatures
4. Promote availability ("Chat with us 24/7!")

### Inform Your Team

Let team members know:
- AI is handling initial inquiries
- How escalation works
- Where to see conversation history
- When they should take over

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "AI gives wrong answers"

**Fix:** Update training information. AI only knows what you teach it.

### "Customers ask for human immediately"

**Fix:** Improve AI's first response to be more helpful. Make escalation available but not the first option.

### "Scheduling doesn't work"

**Fix:** Check calendar connection. Verify availability settings match your actual availability.

### "Responses feel robotic"

**Fix:** Update tone settings. Add more personality to response templates.

### "AI doesn't understand questions"

**Fix:** Add more FAQ variations. Include common misspellings and alternate phrasings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long until I see results?

Most businesses see immediate impact: Faster response times from day one, improved booking rates within the first week.

### What if AI makes a mistake?

You can override any AI action. Monitor closely at first, and AI learns from corrections.

### Can I customize everything?

Yes. Responses, timing, escalation rules, and appearance are all configurable.

### Do I need technical skills?

No. Modern platforms are designed for business owners. If you can write emails, you can configure AI.

### What's the learning curve?

Basic setup: 1-2 hours. Comfortable with all features: 1-2 weeks. Fully optimized: 1-2 months.
