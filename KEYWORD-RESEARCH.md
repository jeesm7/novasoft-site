## Content Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (This Sprint)
1. Pillar content on main topics (5 articles, 1500+ words each)
2. Supporting content for each pillar (10 articles, 800-1200 words each)
3. Internal linking strategy connecting all content

### Phase 2: Expansion (Next 30 Days)
1. Industry-specific landing pages (already have programmatic SEO for this)
2. Case study articles
3. Comparison articles (us vs competitors)

### Phase 3: Authority Building (60-90 Days)
1. Expert roundup posts
2. Original research/surveys
3. Tool/template downloads (link magnets)

## On-Page SEO Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Primary keyword in meta description
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2s
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 other articles
- [ ] External links to 2-3 authoritative sources
- [ ] FAQ section with schema markup
- [ ] Image alt tags with keywords
- [ ] URL slug contains primary keyword
- [ ] 1500+ words for pillar, 800+ for supporting
