## Important Notes

1. **Quality over quantity** - One DA 50+ link > 10 DA 10 links
2. **Natural anchor text** - Mix of branded, URL, and keyword anchors
3. **Relevance matters** - AI/business directories better than random
4. **Patience** - Many links take weeks to be indexed
5. **Track everything** - Use this document to monitor progress
